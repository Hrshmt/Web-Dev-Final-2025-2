Our Mission

BRICS seeks to:

  • Promote sustainable economic development and reduce reliance on traditional Western-centric institutions.
  • Foster multilateral trade and investment among its members and global partners.
  • Champion reforms in global governance structures, including the United Nations and international financial institutions, to better reflect the voices of emerging economies.
  • Address pressing global challenges such as climate change, technological advancement, and poverty alleviation.
